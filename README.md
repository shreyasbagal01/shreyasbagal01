### 🎯 &nbsp; The five things I believe about marketing

1. **Specificity beats clever.** Named entities and hard numbers, every time.
2. **Three lanes, ruthlessly held.** Most brands dilute themselves trying to do five.
3. **Screenshots or it didn't happen.** Every number on my site is verifiable inside the platform-native dashboard.
4. **Personal branding is now a B2B channel.** Founders who show up online win the business their hidden peers do not.
5. **AI search matters as much as Google.** If you're not in ChatGPT and Perplexity, you're invisible to a growing slice of buyers.
